Early life




Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1982[a] in a Telugu family to film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala in Madras (present-day Chennai). His paternal grandfather was the noted film comedian Allu Ramalingaiah who appeared in over 1000 films.[15][16] Their native place is Palakollu of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh.[17] Allu Arjun grew up in Chennai before their family moved to Hyderabad in his 20s.[18][19]

He is the second of three children. His elder brother Venkatesh is a businessman while his younger brother Sirish is also an actor. His paternal aunt is Surekha Konidela, the wife of actor Chiranjeevi.[20] He is the first cousin of actor Ram Charan.[21]
